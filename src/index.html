FUEL Summer Fellows

At Fairfield FUEL our mission is to connect our participants with investors and mentors to help them kick start their companies. When two individuals meet one another in today's tech savvy world, the exchange eventually results in the exchange of social media accounts. However, this process can take quite a while, due to time constraints, complexity and awkwardness. Zapp is the solution for this.
